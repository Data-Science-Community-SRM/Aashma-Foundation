Who are we

Aashma Foundation is a Non-Governmental Organisation in Raipur, Chhattisgarh established on [Date}] with the mission of the upliftment of the society and the propogation of the Humanitarian Message.


Our Vision

We are a group of individuals with humanity in our hearts and a common vision in our mind- Maanav Seva Aashma Tak: Service Beyond Skies. Every person on this planet carries a dream in their eyes and it is the responsibility of all of us to assist those who don't have the wings to fly. Hence, we believe in spreading global kindness beyond skies, because the sky is the limit.

Initiatives

Mask Distribution Drive

Our founder, Reena Verma distributed masks and food supplies amongst the Gas and Vegetable Hawkers after being contacted by them about the neglect they faced from the society. With this, she also urged people to help the ones in need who work everyday to sell them their household supplies.

Acupuncture Campaign

AMF collaborated with the Accupressure Treatment and Training center to organise a free Accupressure and Naturopathy Camp from 26th to 29th February, 2020. The camp provided diagnoses as well as information to the people on the building and maintenance of their physical health.

Food Distribution Campaign

Food along with agricultural items were distributed to the poor and needy people by the foundation. The aim of this initiative was to help people in this difficult time of Coronavirus and also to empower them to earn a better living.
