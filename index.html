Our Vision

We are a group of individuals with humanity in our hearts and a common vision in our mind- Maanav Seva Aash-Maa Tak: Service Beyond Skies. Every person on this planet carries a dream in their eyes and it is the responsibility of all of us to assist those who don't have the wings to fly. Hence, we believe in spreading global kindness beyond skies, because the sky is the limit.

Initiatives

Mask Distribution Drive

Our founder, Reena Verma distributed masks and food supplies amongst the Gas and Vegetable Hawkers after being contacted by them about the neglect they faced from the society. With this, she also urged people to help the ones in need who work everyday to sell them their household supplies.

Acupuncture Campaign

AMF collaborated with the Accupressure Treatment and Training center to organise a free Accupressure and Naturopathy Camp from 26th to 29th February, 2020. The camp provided diagnoses as well as information to the people on the building and maintenance of their physical health.
